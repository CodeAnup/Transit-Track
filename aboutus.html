Welcome to NMAMIT's Bus Route Portal! We're a team of five first-year CSE students, passionate about simplifying campus transportation. This project is our first-year internship endeavor, aimed at providing easy access to all campus bus routes. Our user-friendly website enables students, faculty, and staff to plan commutes, check schedules, and stay updated on route changes. Through collaboration and innovation, we're dedicated to enhancing campus life. This initiative marks our foray into software development and problem-solving. We invite you to explore our platform, share feedback, and join us on this exciting journey. Thank you for being part of our endeavor!
